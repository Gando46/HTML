The best homemade Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese typical food. It consists of a base of a broth (usually chicken) and some noodles.

You usually finish it with some toppings on top.

Ingredients

  • Noodles (1 pack)
  • Broth (Chicken is the most used one)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Your favourite Vegies (Kale works really well)
  • Soft boiled egg
  • Chili oil
  1. First, we have to fry the aromatics in a pan, all together.
  2. Then, prepare the broth (or skip this step if you bought it)
  3. Boil the noodles, and use the same water to boil the egg, but don't overcook it!
  4. Assemble the broth with the noodles, you can accomodate the noodles
  5. Prepare your topings, if you want, you can stir the veggies and the mushrooms
  6. Add all the topping to the soup
  7. Enjoy your delicious Ramen!!
