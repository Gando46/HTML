The best homemade Ramen

Make this spicy, warming Korean comfort food using pork belly and mature kimchi for maximum flavour.

Ingredients

  • 400g mature kimchi
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • 200g of pork belly
  • Gochugaru
  • Caster sugar
  • Tofu
  • Spring onions
  1. First, turn on the heat and start cooking the onions with the vegetable oil.
  2. Then, add the pork belly and the kimchi
  3. After 5 minutes, you can add to the pan the caster sugar and the gochugaru
  4. Meanwhile you can cut the tofu in 5cmx1cm rectangles
  5. After some 5 more minutes, add the tofu with some water
  6. Finaly, add some spring onions to decorate
  7. Enjoy your delicious Kimchi Soup!!
